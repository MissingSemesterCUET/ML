## Overview

Data without visualization is just numbers. This lecture introduces Matplotlib — the foundational plotting library in Python — and Seaborn, which builds on top of it to make statistical visualizations beautiful and effortless. You'll learn to tell stories with your data through charts, distributions, and heatmaps.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture you will be able to:

1. **Create** line plots, scatter plots, bar charts, and histograms using Matplotlib.
2. **Customize** plots with titles, labels, legends, colors, and styles.
3. **Use** subplots and figure layouts to compose multi-panel visualizations.
4. **Build** statistical plots with Seaborn — distribution plots, box plots, violin plots, and pair plots.
5. **Apply** Seaborn themes and color palettes for publication-quality figures.
