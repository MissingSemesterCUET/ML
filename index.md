## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this course, we recommend:

*   **Python Proficiency:** Familiarity with basic syntax, data structures, and libraries like NumPy.
*   **Mathematics:** Basic understanding of linear algebra, calculus, and probability.
*   **Curiosity:** A desire to understand how data can be transformed into insights.
